# Screenshots Guide

## What Screenshots to Take

### BEFORE Screenshots (from original FOSSEE repo)

Go to: https://github.com/FOSSEE/workshop_booking

Take screenshots and save as:

1. `before-home-desktop.png` - Home page (desktop)
2. `before-home-mobile.png` - Home page (mobile, 375px)
3. `before-workshops.png` - Workshop listing (if exists)
4. `before-form.png` - Booking form (if exists)

### AFTER Screenshots (from your app at localhost:3000)

**Desktop (1920x1080):**

1. `after-home-desktop.png` - Home page
2. `after-workshops-desktop.png` - Workshops page
3. `after-detail-desktop.png` - Workshop detail page
4. `after-form-desktop.png` - Booking form

**Mobile (375x667 - iPhone SE):**

1. `after-home-mobile.png` - Home page
2. `after-workshops-mobile.png` - Workshops page
3. `after-form-mobile.png` - Booking form

## How to Take Screenshots

### Desktop Screenshots

1. Open Chrome
2. Go to the page
3. Press **F12** (open DevTools)
4. Press **Ctrl+Shift+P** (Mac: Cmd+Shift+P)
5. Type: "Capture full size screenshot"
6. Press Enter
7. Save to `docs/screenshots/`

### Mobile Screenshots

1. Open Chrome
2. Go to the page
3. Press **F12** (open DevTools)
4. Click **Device Toolbar** icon (or Ctrl+Shift+M)
5. Select **"iPhone SE"** from dropdown
6. Press **Ctrl+Shift+P** (Mac: Cmd+Shift+P)
7. Type: "Capture screenshot"
8. Press Enter
9. Save to `docs/screenshots/`
